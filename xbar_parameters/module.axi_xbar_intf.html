Module axi_xbar_intf

This is the interface wrapper for axi_xbar. Ports and parameters are analog to axi_xbar, see axi_xbar documentation. The AXI4+ATOP master and slave ports are structured here as interfaces.

The indexing of the master and slave port interface arrays is big-endian.
